Child Safety Standards

Last updated: February 25, 2026

At Zyntex Network, the safety of all users, especially minors, is our highest priority. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form on our platform.

1. Our Commitment

Zyntex Network is committed to providing a safe digital environment. We strictly prohibit:

  • Any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, or harms children.
  • Sharing, distributing, or promoting child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form.
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any inappropriate contact with minors.
  • Any activity that facilitates child exploitation, whether directly or indirectly.

2. Age Requirements

Users must be at least 13 years of age to create a Zyntex account. We enforce this through our registration and KYC (Know Your Customer) identity verification process, which requires users to provide valid identification and date of birth.

3. Prevention Measures

We employ the following measures to protect children on our platform:

  • Identity Verification: Our KYC process requires government-issued ID and date of birth verification to confirm user age.
  • Content Monitoring: All user-generated content in live chat and support channels is actively monitored by our admin team for policy violations.
  • Account Enforcement: Accounts found violating child safety standards are immediately suspended, permanently banned, and reported to the appropriate authorities.
  • Staff Training: Our team is trained to identify and respond to potential child safety concerns promptly and effectively.

4. Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any child safety concerns within Zyntex Network, please report them immediately through any of the following channels:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the Live Chat support feature to report concerns directly to our admin team.
  • Email: Send a report to support@zyntexnetwork.com with the subject line "Child Safety Report".

All reports are treated with the highest priority, urgency, and confidentiality. We aim to review and act on every report within 24 hours.

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Zyntex Network fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations. We report all identified CSAM and child exploitation activity to:

  • The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • Relevant regional and national law enforcement authorities.
  • Any other applicable regulatory bodies as required by local law.

6. Data Handling for Minors

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has created an account, we will immediately delete the account and all associated personal data. Parents or guardians who believe their child may have provided personal information can contact us for prompt removal.

7. Continuous Improvement

We regularly review and update our child safety practices to align with the latest industry standards, legal requirements, and best practices. This includes periodic audits of our safety measures and updating our policies as needed.

Questions about child safety?
Contact our team at support@zyntexnetwork.com. We take every concern seriously.
